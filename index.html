Welcome to our Wonderland

Nested in the Yaya Center, Pizza Wonderland is the most premium pizzeria in Nairobi. We offer robust flavours of pizza catering for every palette at affordable prices. With our fast and efficient deliveries our, customers are guaranteed delicious freshly baked pizza at a click of a button. We have been voted the best pizzeria in Kenya for two years in a row and are appreciated for sourcing 80% of our ingredients locally.
